\section{Introduction}

Congestion Control Algorithms are an important topic in nowadays network technology.
They are a necessary tool to control the network flow and to ensure the best use of network resources.
In the following we are going to give a the necessary background to understand Congestion Control Algorithms and corresponding measuring techniques in \ref{sec:Background}.
Then we will show some of the newest Congestion Control Algorithms in \ref{sec:RelatedWork}.
After this we will evaluate and compare them to each other considering the information they provide over their algorithms in \ref{sec:Evaluation}.
